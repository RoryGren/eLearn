Welcome to ii-eLearning

This is the Instruction Screen for your training. Please read through it carefully to help you get the most out of your course.

  • We suggest you maximise your browser to get the best value from your course.
  • This page can be accessed by clicking the button on the top menu.
  • To select a section to start, click the black button on the left hand Navigation Menu and then click the Section you wish to start.
  • The video, if available, will display with its controls. It will NOT start playing automatically and will require you to start it manually.
    Please note that the video might take a few minutes to download from the Internet before it can be viewed, depending on your connection speed. Please be patient.
  • To start or pause the video, click the button or click on the video itself.
  • There is a / button on the right hand side of the screen.
    This will toggle between the video and the transcript of the current section in the course.
  • The videos and transcripts can be viewed as many times as desired, even when marked as complete.
  • For convenience, when logging in, and when clicking on the menu, you will be taken to your last accessed section and video.

Colours

  • Sections that have been viewed will have green text.
  • indicates the section last accessed during the user's previous session.
  • indicates a section that has been completed, but the assessment has not been completed.
