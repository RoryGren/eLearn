Building your BOM

This is all done from the main screen in the Projects Module.

The left hand side of the screen contains the projects and versions - the workspace where we will work, modify and manipulate our BOM.

The right hand side of the screen contains the templates for all the potential material and structure configurations and combinations that could be needed for our BOM.

Remembering that PowerOffice works on selected records, the project version that has been created and is to be used for our BOM needs to be visible, so we double-click on the relevant project and, if necessary, sub-projects, until the version becomes visible. (The Version is where the "nuts and bolts" of our project will be contained.)
