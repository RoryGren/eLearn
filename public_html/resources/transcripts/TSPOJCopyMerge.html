Copying and Merging

Copy and Paste

We know that there is no Copy / Paste on the templates pane, the right hand side of the screen. However, we can copy and paste or drag and drop on the left hand side of the screen, inside our projects and versions.

Copying packages, items and entire versions from one project, or version, to another can be done in standard Windows fashion:

  • Right click the record you want to copy.
    Select Copy in the pop-up menu.
    Right click the new, parent location where you want to paste.
    Select Paste.
  • Highlight the record you want to copy.
    Drag it to the new, parent location where you want to put it.
    Drop it.

PowerOffice will change the pointer icon to a No-Entry sign when the "receiving" record is not valid.
