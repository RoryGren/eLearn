Modifying your Project

Quantities can be modified in two ways:

  1. Right click the relevant record - package or end-item - select Edit and, in the dialog box, change the quantity to the desired number and click OK.
  2. Click twice slowly, not double-click, on the actual quantity you want to change in the grid. PowerOffice will open the quantity for changing in a little block, and then pressing Enter writes the change into the database.
Removing a package or end-item from the BOM
  • In the project hierarchy there is a ticked check box to the left of every record. Removing the tick marks the relevant record as inactive and changes the ticks to grey blocks in the parent container record all the way up the hierarchy to the top-level package in the Version. This grey block and un-ticked block then serves as a record of changes to what was the "standard template," which can be reversed, or added back, simply by putting the tick back into the box.
  • A second way of removing something from the BOM is by right-clicking the relevant record and selecting Delete.
    Warning: This second method cannot be undone. It also has no indication as to what has been changed from the standard template. Use with caution.
